Project OverviewThis project involves the development of a comprehensive Supply Chain Business Intelligence (BI) suite for Motorola. It transforms fragmented e-commerce and logistics data into a centralized, high-performance Power BI environment designed to optimize operational efficiency and executive decision-making.The dashboard provides a "Single Source of Truth" by integrating multi-source data to track the full lifecycle of product movement, from inventory arrival to last-mile delivery.Key Features & InsightsEnd-to-End Tracking: Real-time visibility into critical supply chain KPIs, including RTO (Return to Origin), Inventory Aging, and Last-Mile Delivery Performance.Executive Decision Support: Designed specifically for C-suite and senior management to identify operational bottlenecks and process gaps.Operational Efficiency: Successfully identified ~15% in potential logistics cost-savings and improved backend efficiency by 20%.Performance Optimization: Reduced reporting turnaround time (TAT) by 30% through automated workflows.Technical StackPower BI: Advanced data modeling using Star Schema and interactive visualization.Advanced DAX: Engineered complex measures utilizing Time Intelligence, Iterators, and Filter Context to calculate dynamic growth and efficiency metrics.SQL Server: Developed robust stored procedures and queries to extract and transform large-scale logistics datasets.Power Query (M): Automated ETL processes to clean and consolidate data from 5+ backend systems, eliminating manual reconciliation.Impact & ResultsAutomated Manual Labor: Eliminated manual data entry, saving 10+ hours per week for the supply chain team.Enhanced Data Integrity: Implemented rigorous data validation protocols to ensure 100% accuracy across global reporting units.Strategic Insights: Enabled predictive decision-making by visualizing inventory trends and delivery success rates.
